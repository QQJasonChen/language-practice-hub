EchoLab — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Overview

EchoLab ("the App") is a language learning tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

  • No account registration required
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or shared
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Learning progress (which sentences you've practiced)
  • Bookmarked sentences
  • Spaced repetition review schedule
  • App preferences (theme, playback speed)
  • OpenAI API key (if you choose to enter one for AI explanations)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

If you use the AI Sentence Explain feature, your sentence text is sent to OpenAI's API using your own API key. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
