## Description
EchoLab transforms YouTube videos into powerful language practice sessions. Whether you're learning Dutch, Japanese, English, or any other language — just paste a video URL and start practicing.

**Echo Method (迴音法)**
Based on Professor Shih Jia-Ling's proven technique:
1. 🎧 Listen — Hear the sentence
2. 🧠 Echo — Let it resonate in your mind
3. 🗣️ Repeat — Say it out loud
Each phase is timed with visual progress rings for focused practice.

**AB Loop Player**
Set precise loop points on any sentence. Practice difficult phrases until they become natural. One-tap sentence repeat with word-level precise timestamps powered by Whisper AI.

**Smart Features**
• 💡 AI Sentence Explain — Tap any sentence for instant word-by-word breakdown, grammar notes, and pronunciation tips
• 👂 Dictation Mode — Hide text, listen first, reveal to check
• 🔖 Bookmarks — Mark sentences you want to revisit
• 🔄 Spaced Repetition — Automatic review scheduling based on difficulty
• 📊 Progress Tracking — See your learning stats across all videos
• 🐢🐇 Speed Control — Slow down for beginners, speed up for challenge

**Multi-Language Support**
Works with any language: Dutch, Japanese, English, German, French, Spanish, Korean, Italian, Portuguese, and more. All translations in your native language.

**Precise Audio Sync**
Powered by OpenAI Whisper with word-level timestamps. Every sentence is precisely aligned — no drift, no lag.

Start your language journey today with EchoLab.
